Introduction

This App for Android 6.0 Marshmallow and later (Android 15) features how to read RSS stream from one website, and does some complex Web scraping. It renders the items in the feed directly in native Android components, such as TextView, ImageView

This App was also built to be as fastest and simple as possible. Material design guidelines were applied so you'll find:

  • A menu with your favorites and all categories
  • The ability to refresh the feed with "Pull to refresh"
  • And to customize the feeds to display
  • In the settings, a dark mode or a light mode

Requirements

  • A smartphone at least on Android Marshmallow (6.0)
